Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

KeepClose is designed to help families stay connected through scheduled voice reminders. Your privacy is fundamental to how the app works.

Audio Messages

Voice recordings are stored only long enough to deliver the reminder to the intended recipient.

  • Messages are encrypted during upload, storage, and playback.
  • Audio is automatically deleted immediately after it is played.
  • If a message is not played, it is automatically removed after a short system-defined period.
  • KeepClose does not maintain an archive of recordings.

Data We Collect

We collect only the minimal information required to operate the service, such as account details and reminder scheduling metadata.

What We Do NOT Do

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not use recordings for advertising.
  • We do not analyze or train AI models using your voice.
  • We do not create behavioral profiles.

Security

All transmissions are secured using industry-standard encryption.
